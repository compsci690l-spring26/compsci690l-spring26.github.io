COMPSCI 690L: Advanced Topics/Generative AI

Instructor: Chuang Gan
chuangg@umass.edu
Teaching Assistants (TA):
Sunli Chen
sunlichen@umass.edu

This graduate-level course provides an in-depth exploration of state-of-the-art techniques and foundational models in Generative Artificial Intelligence. Students will study the theoretical underpinnings and practical applications of major generative AI, including LLM, VLM, World Models, Agentic AI, and so on. The course structure emphasizes critical reading of research papers, active discussion, and a culminating research project.

Final Research Project

The final project (40% of final grade) is the major component of this course, allowing students to explore a topic of interest in depth. It can be a theoretical analysis, an original model implementation, or an application of advanced generative techniques. The project is completed in groups of 3 and culminates in a final presentation.

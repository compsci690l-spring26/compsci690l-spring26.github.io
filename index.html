This course will introduce the fundamental techniques and technologies in machine learning. We will cover the basic concepts of machine learning, including supervised and unsupervised learning, statistical learning theory, and deep learning. We will also discuss the applications of machine learning in various fields, such as computer vision and natural language processing.
